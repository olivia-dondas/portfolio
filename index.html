BloomPetals

BloomPetals is a passionate and creative company specializing in designing beautiful floral bouquets to enhance your special events. Whether it's a wedding, birthday, reception, or any other festive occasion, BloomPetals ensures to add a touch of elegance to your celebration.

For this project, I was tasked with creating the front-end design of BloomPetals' website based on a provided mockup. The project includes the following pages:

  • Homepage (index.html):
    • A header with navigation links to the company, services, and contact pages.
    • A prominent title displaying the company name.
    • A brief description of the company, highlighting its expertise in creating stunning floral arrangements for special events.
    • An overview of the three main services offered, each with a title and a short description.
  • Company Page (entreprise.html):
    • A creative and engaging design featuring the company team, including team member photos and descriptions.
    • Emphasis on visually appealing layout and presentation.
  • Contact Page (contact.html):
    • A form allowing potential clients to get in touch with the company.
    • Includes a dropdown for selecting gender, fields for name and address, and a checkbox for agreeing to the privacy policy.
  • Realisations Page (realisations.html):
    • A grid or flexbox-based layout showcasing BloomPetals’ previous floral designs, with a visually appealing mosaic of images.

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Flexbox/Grid (for layout)
  • Responsive Design: Ensuring that all pages are optimized for different screen sizes and devices, providing an enjoyable user experience.

This project allowed me to apply my web development skills, from creating structured HTML pages to crafting responsive designs, and it gave me the opportunity to create a visually engaging experience for the user.
